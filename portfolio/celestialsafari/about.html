Stargazing with us..

Our Astronomers will take you through our galaxy show you the wonders of the night sky and all the beauty it holds. We guarantee amazement and the wow factor with stunning views of our galaxy and our neighbours - Andromeda Galaxy, Leo Triplets, Juniper or Saturn.

Whatever is available to see at the time of the booking. We can cater for groups up to 8 people. Usually our bookings are for 1 - 2 hours, but we also do all night events with breakfast in the morning at our beautiful-secluded. observational site - Starry Sky Farm, situated on the hilltop on the border of Wiltshire and Oxfordshire, near Malborough.

Not only will you see the view through the telescope eyepiece, but we will also track the celestial objects using our camera equipment GoTo scopes and we will share the photos with you so you can remember your magical experience.

On your chosen date, you will be met at our site by one of our Astronomers who will begin your Celestial Safari.

Should you have a specific request to see a certain celestial. object, let us know and we will work out the best time to view it and let you know the dates and times.
